Painted Lady

Echeveria derenbergii are popular low growing ornamental garden plants. Although they are fairly drought tolerant, they will become more spectacular with regular deep watering and fertilising. The hybrids tend to be less tolerant of frost and shade. In temperate climates, most species will lose their lower leaves in winter and become 'leggy' and less attractive.

Echeveria are also popular pot plants and appear in most succulent collections. Echeveria derenbergii is an evergreen perennial succulent with a basal rosette of pagoda-shaped, frosted, fleshy leaves with bristly tips. In Winter it bears clusters of bell-shaped yellow flowers with red tips
