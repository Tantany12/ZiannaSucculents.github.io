Tips for Growing Succulents Anywhere.

Succulents are very popular plants. They’ve got gorgeous shapes and colors, plus they’re drought tolerant. But not everywhere is the ideal place for succulents to grow. These tips will help you keep your succulents alive no matter where you live!

You’re in the right place to learn how to grow succulents–wherever you live!

You’re here because you love succulents, but you’re not sure if you can grow them in your local environment.

Pick the right plants

One of the best pieces of advice I can give you to help your succulents succeed and to help you get your watering schedule “just right” is to record when you’ve watered.

Knowing when you’ve watered last will help answer a lot of questions about whether or not it’s time to water again.

There’s a lot of great ways to keep track of this — pen and paper, excel spreadsheet, notes on your phone — but my favorite way is to use the Succulent Tracker App (iOS only right now). It allows you to easily record each time you water, shows your watering history for each plant, reminds you when it’s time to water, and allows you to keep a photo history of the plant too.
